Storyline

1973 Kombai Village Forest: A despicable DSP Rathna Kumar is assigned to nab the notorious elephant poacher, Shettani. The police torture the local tribals about Shettani's whereabouts, even though they themselves are Shettani's victims.

1973 Madras: Kirubakaran is selected for the post of SI in the Tamil Nadu Police. He goes to his fiancé, Lourde's college, to convey the news and watch her performance at the college culturals. While he's talking with her, they observe a group of students fighting amongst themselves. Lourde challenges Kirubakaran to stop them fighting, to which he replies that he will do that only after he gets his police uniform. But, he goes ahead and tries to stop them anyway. Some time later, 4 of the students are shown to be murdered by Kirubakaran, who seemingly faints at the sight of blood, but has the weapon in his hand. He is then sentenced to prison.

1975 Madurai: Rathna Kumar's elder brother, Jeyakodi, is a film star and rising politician of the ruling party, who is ambitious and desires to be the next Chief minister. However,he is distressed about his film screenings being halted in Coimbatore,Ramnad,Madurai and Tirunelveli by the supporters of his fellow party member and rival Karmegam. Alliyus Caesar, a notorious gangster and head of the Jigarthanda Sambhavam Fans Club is Karmegam's supporter at Madurai. Jeyakodi requests Rathna to kill Alliyus and the 3 other gangsters who are supporting Karmegam's political ambitions, so that he can make the latter less influential within the party. Rathna selects Kirubakaran, along with three other ex-cop prisoners, to send them as moles and assigns each of them a single target, with Kirubakaran's target being Alliyus. Rathna promises that all four of them will be acquitted and resumed to their duties if they succeed in their respective mission.

Kirubakaran accepts the assignment as he wants to marry Lourde and get back his job. Espoused by another of Karmegam's supporters during a party, Alliyus advertises publicly in search for a film director who can make a pan-India film with him as the lead and introduce him as the first dark-complexioned actor in Tamil cinema. Kirubakaran assumes the alias of Ray Dasan, a student and assistant director of Satyajit Ray, and offers to make an Oscar-winning film based on Alliyus's life, as a gangster film, titled Pandyaa, after learning that he is an ardent fan of Clint Eastwood. Alliyus agrees to Ray's plan and their attempt to make a film. Kirubakaran wants to make a biopic of Alliyus and does live-shooting of his real life actions using a Super 8 film camera that Clint Eastwood apparently gifted to Caesar when he came for a shoot in their village. Kirubakaran learns that Alliyus belongs to the same tribe that is being haunted by Shettani. It is shown that Alliyus left the village as a young boy with his father due to a tragic incident and became a gangster as he lost faith in being good and empathetic.

Alliyus invites the other 3 gangsters and Karmegam for his wife, Malaiyarasi's baby shower. One of the targets in the police' hitlist is successfully killed by one of the moles. This raises suspicion and Alliyus finds and kills all of the moles, except Kirubakaran, as the latter is unsuspected. He also learns that Alliyus ordered the hit on the 4 college students, for which he was made a scapegoat. This revelation hardens his determination to pursue vengeance against Alliyus. He wants Alliyus to be killed by Shettani, and hence asks him to visit his ancestral village and nab Shettani with the pretext that his adventure against Shettani will make a better biopic. Alliyus unwittingly agrees and visits the village and makes a deal with Rathna that he would nab Shettani in exchange of releasing the tribal members, to which the latter agrees.
